A vulnerable road user is considered invisible if a driver cannot see their head and shoulders. The shoulder heights of 5th percentile United States female vulnerable road users are used in this calculation.

By each type of VRU type in the VIEW app, the height to shoulder measurements are: preschool child – 28 inches, elementary school child – 37 inches, elementary school child on bicycle – 35 inches, wheelchair user – 39 inches, adult on bicycle – 47 inches, and adult – 49 inches.
