The individual data points on the left show specific vehicles entered into the database. The gray curves to the right show histograms for each body class. Histograms show what percentage of data points (in this case, visibility scores) fall in any range of values. Body class is a convenient way to separate vehicles by purpose - categories include passenger vehicles, SUVs, minivans, vans, pickup trucks, commercial trucks, and buses. These are each pulled using VINs or vehicle make/models from entries.
